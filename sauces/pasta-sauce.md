# Pasta Sauce

## Ingredience

- 400 g Pasata
- 200 g Mince
- 2 Onions
- 8 Cloves Garlic  
- 2 tsp Salt
- 1 Stock Cube

## Steps

### Step 1

Chop onions and garlic into cubes.

### Step 2

Fry onions and and garlic lightly

### Step 3

Add your mince. Fry until brown.

### Step 4

Fold in Salt and Stock

### Step 5

Add Pasata. Bring to light boil
