# Homemade Heinz-style Ketchup

## Ingredience

- 400g Tomato paste 
- 400g Glucose syrup 
- 400g Distilled vinegar 
- 80g Sugar
- 30g Salt 
- 0.8g Clove, ground 
- 2g MSG, optional 
- 2.4g Xanthan gum 
- 1 sprinkle of pepper 

## Steps

### Step 1

Combine all ingredients in a blender. Blend until the mixture forms a smooth and homogeneous pur√©e.

## Tips

Adding tomato paste and vinegar before dumping in the glucose syrup will prevent the syrup from sticking to the bottom of the blender.
