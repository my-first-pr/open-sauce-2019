# Hot Sauce

> 'Hot sauce is good' - Alexander the Great

## Ingredients:

1. 400g *tinned tomatoes*
2. 1Tbs *hot chili powder*
3. 2Tbs *chopped chilies*
4. A splash of *lemon*
5. 1Tsp *cumin*

## Method:

Put **all** the ingredients into a blender and blend.

Use as a marinade with **chicken** or **pork**.
